We are the team of Maximo technical and business consultants based in Abu Dhabi, UAE. We have fifteen years of experience providing solutions for companies based in the UAE and throughout the Middle East.

We serviced clients in a wide range of industries: Oil and Gas, Transportation, Government and Military, Energy Sector. Our team provide expertise in business analysis and consulting, Maximo application configuration and development, Maximo workflow and business process design, Maximo Integration (with SAP, Oracle applications or custom applications), custom Maximo modules and components design, Maximo Mobile development and building the satellite applications outside of Maximo and ERP systems.

While working on the Maximo Mobile and kiosk-based projects, we faced challenges with the existing tools and methodologies, both from the Maximo toolkit or third party solutions. We needed something to accommodate even the most demanding client requirements and still finish the projects in a short time. As a result of our efforts and the experience we got while implementing the Maximo based mobile application, the MaximoPlus platform was born.

For any questions about the MaximoPlus, demo, or POC request, please write to us at info@maximoplus.com.
