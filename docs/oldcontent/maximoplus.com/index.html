For better Maximo Mobile Apps

Empower your team with the easiest and the most powerful Maximo Mobile solution. Build any type of Maximo Mobile app, and make it look and behave the way you want to.

Simplicity

Drastically reduce development and deployment time and cost, and build the apps with the much better user experience. Handful of javascript lines is all it takes to get a simple app up and running. No need for Maximo module installations, script running, MIF and web services.

Extendible

Ready made Componenents directly bound to Maximo, and fully open for control. Components are familiar to any Maximo develpper: Grid, Section, Workflow, GL, Lookups and much more. Just pick the bits you need, plugin to Maximo and make them work they way you want.

Customizable

Use any of the existing mobile libraries or frameworks from the market and adapt it easily to MaximoPlus. Ready made templates for several frameworks are included to help you get started.

Batteries included

Custom built server, Maximo ear file and the working database is all you need for setup. No additional installations, no third party servers, no expensive infrastructure, no Maximo setup of any kind is required

Focus on the UI

Everything else is handled by MaximoPlus. Think about the navigation, scrolling, animations and everything else that makes the great mobile app. Build the custom controls and give your users sleek and contemporary mobile experience.

Automatic Offline Support

Pick what you want to be saved offline, and the synchronization is done automatically. Even the offline Maximo workflow is supported.

Powerful Exception Handling

Control exactly what happens when the error comes from Maximo.

Don't lag

Why your app has to look old fashioned and ugly? Choose the bleeding edge look and feel from the market leading HTML5/CSS libraries and plug it in. Just find the one you like on the Internet, and integrate it easilly with MaximoPlus. Examples and templates are included for Framework7, Polymer, Topcoat, Bootstrap...

Direct Access to Device Features

It is trivial to make your app use Maps, Camera, Phone Calls, Barcode scanning, SMS, you name it. Library provide direct access through javascript and Cordova plugins.

Built for Speed

With this blazingly fast front-end library, Maximo back-end will be the only performance bottleneck you'll have

The most cost effective Maximo Mobile Solution

Cut the development time drastically and get the most powerful results. No need for MIF, expensinve third party servers, complicated server side frameworks and offline setup, MaximoPlus handles everything automatically.

Not just for the Mobile

Maybe you need the kiosk app for the people working on sites, or you want to have the Maximo-based app in your company portal. You may also need features that are almost impossible to do in Maximo, like multiple files upload or download. Just make a small MaximoPlus app and integrate it with Maximo through Launch in Context

Upcoming features

In the upcoming version, you will be able to make full Native mobile apps, MaximoPlus will provide integration with the Facebook React Native library. Also, MaximoPlus will support nodejs for the ones that want the Internet of Things solution on nodejs enabled microcontrollers or Raspberry PI
